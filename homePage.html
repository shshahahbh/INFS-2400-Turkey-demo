Understanding Inequality in Turkey

Our project will address the economic inequality in Turkey. With a focus on youth unemployment, homelessness, and women’s rights. It will look at the UN Sustainable Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth, with target 8.6 which aims to reduce youth unemployment and increase education and training for them. Our project will also looks at Goal 1: End poverty in all its forms, with target 1.2, which aims to cut at least by half the proportion of men, women and children of all ages living in poverty. The website project will provide resources, raise awareness, and suggest solutions to help young people find jobs, issue three, and reduce homelessness caused by economic instability.
