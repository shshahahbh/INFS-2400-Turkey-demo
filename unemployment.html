Idle Labor Rate

According to TUIK the data shows that the rate of the total unemployment is about 8.5% in Turkey as of February 2026 and about 15.8% of youth unemployment. TUIK also mentions a “Idle Labor Rate” of about 29.7%, and this basically states the unemployment statistics that included people who actually wanted to work more hours but just weren’t allowed too. This sources also states that females in turkey see more unemployment stats than men.

Beyond Costs

Beyond all the percentages we must realize that nowadays prices on the cost of living, taxes, and overall life of living are so much more expensive now. Most companies don’t want to take young-youth workers because sometimes they don’t have the right experiences. Most of the time it’s the actual person who is lazy and doesn’t want to work and that gives then unemployment, but you have to think about how hard it is to live in Turkey because of all the issues that have arisen there over the years.
