Who Are We?

ABOUT US

Our web development group created this project to bring attention to inequality in Turkey and how it affects people’s everyday lives. We mainly focused on issues like homelessness, gender inequality, and unemployment, and how these problems make it harder for people to have stable living conditions and equal opportunities. For example, homelessness can make it difficult for people to find jobs or access basic needs, while gender inequality can limit opportunities for women in education and the workplace. Unemployment is another major issue that affects many families and makes it harder for people to support themselves.
Even though Turkey has a rich culture and history, many people still face these challenges every day. Through our website, we share information, research, and real-life examples to help others better understand these problems and why they matter. Our goal is to raise awareness, start conversations, and encourage people to think about ways society can become more fair and equal for everyone.
